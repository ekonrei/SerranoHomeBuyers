Privacy Policy

Serrano Familia REI LLC, doing business as SerranoHomeBuyers ("Company," "we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website at serranocashoffers.com or contact us in connection with our services.

By using our website or submitting your information, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Full name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Property Information: Property address, condition, and details you voluntarily provide about your real estate situation.
  • Communication Data: Messages, notes, or other information you provide when contacting us via forms, phone, email, or text.
  • SMS Consent: Whether you have opted in to receive SMS text messages from us, the date and time of consent, and the source of your consent (e.g., our website form).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, referring pages, and other standard website analytics data collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your property inquiry and prepare a cash offer.
  • To communicate with you about your property, including follow-ups, appointment scheduling, and offer updates.
  • To send you SMS text messages if you have provided your consent (see Section 3 below).
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights and safety of our company and others.

3. SMS Communications and Consent

Serrano Familia REI LLC, doing business as SerranoHomeBuyers, may send SMS text messages only after you provide consent through our website form. We collect two separate types of SMS consent, both of which are optional. Users may submit the form without opting in to SMS.

Transactional and Informational SMS: By checking the optional Transactional SMS Consent checkbox on our website form, you consent to receive transactional and informational SMS messages from Serrano Familia REI LLC, doing business as SerranoHomeBuyers, at the phone number provided. These messages are used only for communications related to your submitted property inquiry and may include property inquiry follow-ups, property questions, appointment coordination, scheduling updates, cash offer updates, and communications related to your submitted request. Transactional and informational SMS consent is optional and is collected separately from promotional SMS consent.

Promotional and Marketing SMS: Promotional and marketing SMS messages are optional and are only sent if you separately check the optional Promotional SMS Consent checkbox on our website form. Promotional messages may include promotional home-buying updates, future cash offer opportunities, and related real estate marketing messages. Consent to promotional SMS is not required to submit a form, receive a cash offer, or complete a transaction.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your inquiry and where you are in our process.

Message & Data Rates: Msg & data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-Out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message we send you. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

Help: For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact us at isaacantoniobusy@gmail.com.

Your SMS consent records may include your phone number, the type of consent provided (transactional and/or promotional), the date and time of consent, the form source, and your submitted form data. This information is stored securely in our CRM system and is used solely to manage our communication with you.

SMS opt-in data and consent information, including transactional SMS consent and promotional SMS consent, will not be sold, rented, shared, or disclosed to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Service providers who assist us in operating our website, CRM, and messaging systems may only use this data to operate and deliver those services on our behalf.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, including phone numbers and SMS consent data, with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our website, CRM, and communications infrastructure (e.g., GoHighLevel, email providers). These parties are contractually obligated to keep your data confidential and use it only as directed by us.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Privacy Policy.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information to protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, our customers, or others.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our CRM and communication systems use industry-standard encryption and access controls.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, to comply with legal obligations, or as otherwise required by applicable law. If you request deletion of your information, we will make reasonable efforts to comply, subject to legal and business requirements.
